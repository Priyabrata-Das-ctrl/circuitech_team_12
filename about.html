About Us

We provide innovative solutions for monitoring and improving water quality in real-time...

Core Features :

Real-Time Data Display

Show live updates of sensor readings (turbidity, pH, TDS, etc.) with interactive graphs.

Historical Data Logs

Enable users to view past data trends through downloadable reports or charts.

Multi-Device Access

Ensure the website is mobile-friendly and responsive for access on any device.

Threshold Alerts (coming soon)

Display warning messages on the dashboard for abnormal parameters (e.g., unsafe pH or high turbidity).

Our Vision :

To revolutionize water management by leveraging smart technology, fostering a future where access to clean and safe water is universal, sustainable, and efficiently managed through innovative and scalable solutions.

Our Mission :

To provide an efficient, real-time solution for monitoring water quality by integrating multiple sensors and IoT technology, ensuring accurate measurement of key parameters, promoting sustainable water usage, and safeguarding public health and environmental integrity.
